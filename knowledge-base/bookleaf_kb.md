# BookLeaf Publishing - 21-Day Writing Challenge Knowledge Base

## Getting Started
**Q: How do I join the 21-Day Writing Challenge?**
A: You can register for the 21-Day Writing Challenge on our main website. Once registered and payment is confirmed, you'll receive your dashboard login credentials via email to begin your writing journey.

**Q: What’s included in the ₹1999 publishing package?**
A: The ₹1999 package includes participating in the 21-Day Challenge, access to our writing dashboard, standard cover design templates, ISBN assignment, formatting, and standard distribution on Amazon and Flipkart as a paperback.

**Q: Is this challenge genuine?**
A: Yes! The 21-Day Writing Challenge has helped thousands of aspiring authors publish their first book. We are a registered publishing house and you can find many of our authors' books on Amazon.

**Q: Can I use a pen name?**
A: Absolutely! You can write and publish under a pen name. You can specify your pen name in the author details section of your dashboard before final submission.

**Q: What if I want to publish without joining the challenge?**
A: If you already have a completed manuscript, you can bypass the challenge and opt for our standard publishing packages. Please contact our support team for a custom quote.

## Payments & Refunds
**Q: What does the ₹1999 cover?**
A: It covers the platform fee, ISBN generation, standard cover templates, internal formatting, and basic distribution. Author copies and premium marketing are not included.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I change my mind?**
A: Refunds are only applicable if requested within 24 hours of payment, provided you haven't started using the writing dashboard. Once the challenge begins, the fee is non-refundable.

**Q: Can I upgrade later with add-on services?**
A: Yes! Even after you pay the initial ₹1999, you can purchase add-ons like premium cover design, the ₹8899 premium package, or marketing services from your dashboard.

## Dashboard & Submission
**Q: I didn’t receive my login details — what should I do?**
A: First check your spam/promotions folder. If it's not there, email our support team with your payment screenshot and we will manually trigger the login details.

**Q: I forgot my password — how can I reset it?**
A: Go to the dashboard login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email to receive a reset link.

**Q: How do I submit my poems?**
A: You must log into the daily dashboard, type or paste your poem in the text editor, and click "Submit for Today".

**Q: Can I write in Hindi?**
A: Yes, you can submit your poems in English or Hindi. If writing in Hindi, please use standard Unicode or Mangal font.

**Q: How do I save my progress?**
A: The dashboard auto-saves your drafts. However, always ensure you click "Save Draft" before closing the tab to be safe.

**Q: Can I rearrange my poems later?**
A: Yes, before the final submission on Day 21, you have an option to rearrange the sequence of your poems.

**Q: What happens after I submit all poems?**
A: After your final protocol submission, your manuscript is locked. Our team will review it for formatting and formatting, assign an ISBN, and begin the cover matching process.

**Q: How do I review and finish my submission?**
A: On the dashboard, go to the "Review Manuscript" tab, verify all poems and titles, and click the final "Approve for Publishing" button.

## Cover Design & Book Setup
**Q: How do I design my cover?**
A: Use our built-in Cover Creator Tool on the dashboard. You can select templates, customize colors, and add text.

**Q: Can I upload my own cover design?**
A: Yes, if you have a custom design, you can upload it using the "Custom Upload" option in the Cover Creator. Ensure it meets the specified dimensions.

**Q: Why do I see only 4 templates?**
A: The standard ₹1999 package includes 4 premium templates. For more templates, you can purchase the premium cover add-on.

**Q: What should I write on the back cover?**
A: The back cover usually contains a short blurb about the book and a brief author biography. Keep it engaging!

**Q: Can I upload my profile photo?**
A: Yes, you can upload a high-resolution author photo in the "Author Info" section, which will be printed on the back cover.

**Q: How do I edit or remove the back cover text?**
A: You can edit or remove the back cover text in the "Metadata & Back Cover" section of your dashboard before finalizing submission.

## Publishing & Distribution
**Q: How long does it take to publish my book?**
A: For standard packages, it takes 30-45 days after final submission for the book to go live on platforms.

**Q: What’s the timeline with the ₹8899 add-on?**
A: The ₹8899 expedited add-on fast-tracks your book, bringing the publishing timeline down to 14-21 days.

**Q: Where will my book be available?**
A: Your book will be available for purchase on major platforms including Amazon, Flipkart, and the BookLeaf store.

**Q: What’s the royalty rate?**
A: Standard royalty is 100% of the net profit on each sale. Net profit is MRP minus printing and platform distribution costs.

**Q: When will I get my royalty report?**
A: Royalty reports are generated quarterly (every 3 months) and uploaded directly to your dashboard.

## Policies & Support
**Q: Who owns the rights to my book?**
A: You retain 100% of the copyright and intellectual property rights to your work. We are simply your publishing partner.

**Q: My dashboard isn’t working — what should I do?**
A: Please clear your browser cache, try incognito mode, or switch browsers. If the issue persists, contact technical support.

**Q: Can I submit via email if the portal isn’t loading?**
A: We highly recommend using the portal, but in case of severe technical outages near deadline, email submission to support is temporarily accepted.
